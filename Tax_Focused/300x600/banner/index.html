Qantas Frequent Flyer members who purchase a Qantas Assure Life Protect policy between 05/06/17 and 30/06/17 will earn double sign on Qantas Points. Minimum monthly premium in order to earn Qantas Points is $40. To earn the maximum 100,000 points, minimum monthly premium is $160. Points will credited over 9 months, in 8 equal monthly instalments which will commence after the policy has been held for 60 continuous days. To receive each instalment of Qantas Points, the qualifying premium must remain the same and payments must be up to date.
